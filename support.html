Common questions

How do I apply a watch face?

  • Long-press your current watch face on the watch.
  • Swipe through the gallery and tap the installed watch face.
  • Or open the Wear OS app on your phone and pick it from the “Watch faces” section.

How do I change colors or complications?

  • Long-press the active watch face on the watch, then tap the gear / customize icon.

Does the watch face work offline?

  • Yes. All watch faces run entirely on-device and require no internet connection.
