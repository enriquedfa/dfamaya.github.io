About

A collection of minimal, focused watch faces for Wear OS. Each one is built to stay out of the way — clean layouts, legible time, and the complications you choose to add.

Everything runs on your device. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no data collection — the watch faces only render what Wear OS gives them.

Customizing

  • Long-press the active watch face on your watch, then tap the gear / customize icon.
  • Pick colors, styles, and the complications you want to show.
  • Your choices are stored locally by Wear OS and never leave your device.
